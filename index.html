FAQ

Common questions

Is this free?
Yes. Subtitle Hub is completely free with optional ads to support development. No subscriptions, no hidden charges.
How do I get more than 5 downloads per day?
Create a free account at opensubtitles.com, then log in from Settings → OpenSubtitles Account. You'll get 20 downloads per day using your own quota.
What subtitle formats are supported?
SRT, TXT, VTT, ASS, SSA, and JSON. SRT is recommended for most video players.
Do you store my data?
No. Your search history and favorites are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, track, or sell any personal information.
Why is this not on the Play Store?
Google's policies restrict apps that download YouTube content. The app is perfectly legal — it only downloads subtitles, not videos — but we distribute via APK to avoid policy issues.
How do I update the app?
Download the latest APK from this page and install it over the existing version. Your data and settings will be preserved.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Subtitle Hub ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app processes YouTube URLs and search queries locally on your device and sends them to our backend server solely to fetch subtitle data. No personal information is transmitted or stored beyond what is necessary for the core functionality.

2. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

For movie/TV show subtitles, this app uses the OpenSubtitles.com API. Search queries and language preferences are sent to OpenSubtitles servers to retrieve subtitle files.

3. Data Storage

Subtitle files you download are saved to your device's Downloads folder. App preferences and history are stored locally on your device using Room Database. We do not access or upload these files.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
